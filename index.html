Riding rollercoasters was never an option for me prior to visiting the park. I was delighted when I first saw the highest rollercoaster, "Goliath," which stands 200 feet tall, but as soon as I started making the line, the nerves came in. However, while inside and feeling the G forces, I was shocked at how much pleasure it was to feel adrenaline coursing through my entire body. Six Flags Magic Mountain's rollercoasters are a must-ride!

Six Flags Magic Mountain's food is great, however it is somewhat pricey. I spent over $100 on food during the day, but I had little option because I was starving and they don't allow you to bring food from outside, which is rather harsh. Nonetheless, the meal was delicious, and the service was outstanding!
